General information
1.C-DAC has a feedback system, which is made accessible to all the students during the progress of each module.
2.The feedback of faculty is taken for all the modules through this feedback system.
3.The feedback process is scheduled by the respective centre and the url for the same is shared with the students.
4.The feedback schedule is categorised as Mid module and End module. The frequency of Mid Module feedback ranges from 3 to 5 times, depending on the duration of the respective modules and for the End Module it is taken only once at the end of the module.
Do's
1.Giving the module wise feedback is compulsory.
2.You can log-in anytime anywhere as it is a web based application and give the feedback during the scheduled dates. Note: After the scheduled dates the feedback cannot be given.
3.You should give the feedback independently, specifically on the aspects of teaching.
4.Your Feedback is very important in order to improve the teaching quality and it will make the system robust, so do participate in the process.
Dont's
1. Do not give feedback under the influence of anybody or Institute
2. Personal prejudices should not dictate the feedback. The system is in place to identify such cases.
